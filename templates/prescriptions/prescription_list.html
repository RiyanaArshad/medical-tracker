Medication Tips

  • Take medications at the same time each day
  • Use a pill organizer to keep track of doses
  • Set alarms or reminders on your phone
  • Keep a medication diary or checklist

  • Store medications in their original containers
  • Keep medications away from heat, light, and moisture
  • Check expiration dates regularly
  • Dispose of unused or expired medications properly
  • Keep all medications out of reach of children and pets

  • Report any side effects promptly
  • Inform your doctor about all medications you're taking
  • Ask questions if you don't understand instructions
  • Don't stop taking medication without consulting your doctor
  • Bring a list of all your medications to every appointment
